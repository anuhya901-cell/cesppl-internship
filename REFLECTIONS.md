# REFLECTIONS

## Week 5 - Karpathy and Flowers

### Rule 1: Become One with the Data

Understanding the dataset before training helps identify labeling issues and class imbalances.

### Rule 2: Start Simple and Incrementally Add Complexity

A strong baseline model is more valuable than immediately trying advanced techniques.

### Rule 3: Overfit a Small Batch First

If the model cannot memorize a small sample, there is likely a bug in the training pipeline.

### Rule 4: Visualize Training and Validation Metrics

Graphs reveal overfitting, underfitting, and optimization issues that raw metrics may hide.

### Rule 5: Inspect Misclassified Examples

Looking at incorrect predictions often provides more insight than overall accuracy numbers.
